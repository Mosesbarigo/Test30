Data Management Systems

Inovation has been a vital part of the school where we look at how best we can improve our work processes to archive quality results. This pave way for improvements in the space of technology where we strive to improve and make better some of the things we do.

Out of the few systems we are working on, three (3) are currently being used

1. Asset Register


A simple and effective system that keeps record of all data assets we have in the school. The system has its proceses and proceedures in updating records, ensuring assets records are in order aged assets are disposed and more importantly, budgets on asstes ae re controlled and properly managed.

2. Students Assessment System


Student Assessment system has been the icon for the school where we invest in improving our accademic assessment process to address the need for manual calculation and the associated errors that comes with it. Student Assessment system was developed 2017 and continues to upgrade since then. In 2022 we design the system to capture the SBC standards while at the same time enhenacing user experience by making the system more user friendly and effective.
